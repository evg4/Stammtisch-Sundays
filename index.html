Pull up a stool and grab a beer!

Join us every Sunday for our bilingual get-together. We are a friendly bunch of English and German speakers who meet up every week to practise our language skills over a beer.

FAQs

  1. What if I don't speak German?

    No problem! Some people speak English and German, some people just speak one or the other. Either way, there will be someone you will be able to talk to!

  2. Do I have to come every week?

    This is a casual, drop-in event. You don't have to commit to a certain number of weeks and you don't have to let us know in advance if you're coming. Just turn up!

  3. Does it cost anything?

    Entrance is free! And we don't charge any sort of membership fee. You just have to pay for any food or drinks you'd like on the night.

  4. What if I don't drink alcohol?

    There is absolutely no obligation to drink beer or any other alcohol! Feel free to have whatever you feel comfortable with.
